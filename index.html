Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

Smart PDF Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as names, email addresses, contacts, or documents.

2. Camera Permission

The app requires access to your device camera solely for scanning documents. Images captured are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

3. Gallery Access

When you select images from your device gallery, the app only accesses the selected images to convert them into PDF files. We do not access your entire photo library.

4. Document Processing

PDF generation, image compression, and document processing occur locally on your device using secure browser and device capabilities.

5. AI Features

If you use AI-powered features such as text recognition or translation, selected content may be securely sent to Google Gemini AI services for processing. We do not store this content on our servers.

6. Local Storage

The app may store settings and premium status locally on your device using secure local storage. This data does not leave your device.

7. Internet Access

The app requires internet access for AI processing and feature updates. No personal data is collected or sold.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security practices. Since most processing occurs locally, your data remains under your control.

9. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page.
